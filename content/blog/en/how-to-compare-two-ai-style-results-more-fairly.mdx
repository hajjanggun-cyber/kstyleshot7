## Intro

How to Compare Two AI Style Results More Fairly is a conversion-stage topic: the goal is not to collect many attempts, but to improve one attempt that the model can read clearly.

## Why This Matters

Most weak outputs come from signal noise, not from model quality. If the upload is inconsistent, adding more uploads usually multiplies the same problem.

## Practical Framework

1. Define one target mood before you upload.
2. Keep framing and lighting stable while you compare options.
3. Change one variable at a time so you know what caused improvement.

## Common Mistakes

- Uploading many similar but low-quality selfies
- Mixing different angles and lighting in one decision cycle
- Judging outputs without a fixed comparison baseline
