## Intro

Background Cleanliness Tips Before Uploading a Selfie focuses on the technical side of upload quality: angle, light, distance, and framing are usually the fastest levers for cleaner outputs.

## Why This Matters

Photo technique errors are compounding errors. A small angle mismatch plus flat lighting can make style selection feel inconsistent even when prompts are fine.

## Practical Framework

1. Lock your camera height first (eye-level baseline).
2. Confirm one clean light direction before pressing capture.
3. Keep body crop and face position consistent across retries.

## Common Mistakes

- Adjusting pose before fixing camera geometry
- Retrying in different rooms without resetting lighting
- Cropping too tight and amplifying lens distortion
