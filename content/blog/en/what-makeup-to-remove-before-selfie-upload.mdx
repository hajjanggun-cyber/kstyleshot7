## Intro

What Makeup to Remove Before a Selfie Upload is an SEO-intent article for users who search practical selfie makeup fixes before uploading to an AI style tool.

## Quick Summary

- Remove visual noise before adding more products.
- Keep skin texture readable, not overly covered.
- Validate results under the same light before upload.

## Why This Matters for Style Results

Beauty prep affects the model's input clarity. Heavy or inconsistent makeup often creates unstable facial signals and lowers result consistency.

## Action Steps

1. Reset one area first (base, eyes, or shine), not all at once.
2. Re-check in front-camera mode with neutral expression.
3. Keep one repeatable setup as your default upload prep.
