## Intro

More uploads do not automatically mean better AI style results. In most cases, one clean selfie with stable framing beats five rushed selfies with mixed conditions.

## What "One Good Selfie" Actually Means

A good selfie for style generation is not about looking perfect. It is about giving the model clear, consistent signals.

Minimum quality signals:

1. Face is centered and not cropped too tightly
2. Light direction is clear (not mixed from multiple warm/cool sources)
3. Hairline and jawline are readable without heavy blur
4. Expression is neutral enough for style transfer

## Why Multiple Average Uploads Often Fail

When each upload has different angle, distance, and light, the model sees conflicting inputs. That increases variation and makes comparison hard.

Common pattern:

- Upload 1: good angle, poor light
- Upload 2: good light, distorted distance
- Upload 3: better face, messy background

Result: more files, but no reliable baseline.

## Quick Decision Rule Before Upload

Use this 30-second rule:

1. If one selfie passes all four quality signals, upload only that one first.
2. If none pass, retake once and fix the biggest issue first.
3. Only add a second upload when it is intentionally better, not just different.
