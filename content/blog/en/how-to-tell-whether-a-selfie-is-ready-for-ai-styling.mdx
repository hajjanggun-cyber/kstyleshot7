## Intro

You can avoid many failed generations by checking readiness before upload. A fast pre-upload test is often the highest ROI step in the entire flow.

## The 5-Point Readiness Test

Pass at least 4 out of 5 before uploading:

1. **Angle**: Front-facing or slightly above eye level
2. **Distance**: Face fills frame without wide-angle distortion
3. **Lighting**: One dominant light source, no heavy shadow split
4. **Background**: Simple enough that your face remains the main subject
5. **Hair/Makeup noise**: No detail that overwhelms facial structure

## If You Fail 2+ Points

Do not retry generation yet. Retake first.

Retake priority:

1. Fix lighting
2. Fix distance
3. Fix background
4. Then adjust expression/hair detail

## Why This Works

AI style systems are sensitive to input consistency. Clean readiness checks reduce random variation and make output comparison fair.
