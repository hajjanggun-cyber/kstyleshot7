## Intro

Heavy styling is not always the best strategy for selfie-based AI portraits. Clean hair structure usually performs better than complex texture.

## Why Clean Hair Wins in Practice

The model needs readable face framing. Overstyled hair can hide key contours and add noisy edges.

Cleaner setups improve:

- Forehead-to-bang transition
- Cheek and jawline visibility
- Balance between hair shape and outfit details

## A Low-Effort K-Style Hair Framework

1. Pick one mood: soft, clean, or lightly textured.
2. Keep front and side framing consistent with your camera angle.
3. Avoid combining strong volume + strong fringe + strong texture in one shot.

## Fast Comparison Method

Capture two variants only:

1. Clean base version
2. Slightly styled version

If version 2 reduces face readability, keep version 1 as your default upload hair setup.
