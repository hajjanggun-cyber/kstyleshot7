## Intro

After hair direction is fixed, outfit selection is where most portrait consistency is either secured or lost.

## Why Outfit Matching Matters

SM HYBE JYP Headquarters Area carries a **modern glass textures and sleek lines** atmosphere. Outfits with **modern minimal tailoring with clean edges** keep contrast under control while protecting facial clarity.

## 10 Outfit Directions

1. Neutral monochrome with one texture contrast
2. Tone-on-tone layering with light value separation
3. Structured jacket over clean inner top
4. Soft knit + straight silhouette bottoms
5. Crisp shirt + minimal accessory focus
6. Light outerwear + low-noise color palette

## Scene Matching Tips

- Dense backgrounds pair better with simpler color blocks.
- Calm scenes can handle one stronger accent item.
- Neckline clarity matters more than logo size in selfies.

## Common Mistakes

- Adding too many trend items in one frame
- Using bright accents that compete with face highlights
- Ignoring upper-body crop behavior in 9:16 framing

## Quick Checklist

- Confirm hair direction first
- Pick one core silhouette
- Limit accent colors
- Check neckline and shoulder readability
- Validate outfit in two framing crops
