## Intro

You do not always need a new photo. Sometimes a few quick fixes are enough.

## Why This Matters

Small corrections before upload often improve the result more than changing the style prompts later.

## 4 Fast Fixes

### 1. Improve the light first

Move toward cleaner front light before doing anything else.

### 2. Increase the distance slightly

If the face feels too close, step back and retake with a little more room.

### 3. Remove heavy filters

Use the cleanest source possible so the face shape stays readable.

### 4. Clear the frame

A simple background and cleaner crop reduce noise immediately.

## Common Mistakes

- Editing too much instead of retaking
- Leaving the same weak lighting
- Trying to fix a distorted close-up with cropping alone

## Quick Checklist

- Better light
- Better distance
- Fewer filters
- Cleaner frame
