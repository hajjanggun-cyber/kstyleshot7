## Intro

Flat hair can make even a clean selfie feel less polished than it should.

## Why This Matters

The upload looks stronger when the hair shape has enough lift and separation to read clearly.

## 4 Simple Tricks

### 1. Lift the crown slightly

Even a small amount of height can make the whole portrait feel more balanced.

### 2. Avoid heavy collapse at the sides

Too much flatness near the temples can make the face feel wider and heavier.

### 3. Keep the ends tidy

Volume works best when the lower silhouette still looks controlled.

### 4. Check volume in the same light you will use

Hair shape can look very different under soft front light versus dim indoor light.

## Common Mistakes

- Adding random frizz instead of shape
- Lifting only one side
- Ignoring how lighting changes the silhouette

## Quick Checklist

- Small crown lift
- Controlled sides
- Tidy ends
- Match the lighting setup
