## Intro

How to Pick a Background Before You Pick Hair addresses backdrop-first decision making for users who want cleaner mood consistency in AI portrait workflows.

## Quick Summary

- Backdrop choice can reduce later styling conflict.
- Keep face visibility as the first criterion.
- Use mood consistency across hair, outfit, and scene.

## Why This Matters for Composition Quality

Backdrop mood controls contrast, color temperature, and visual density. If it is chosen late, users often over-correct hair or outfit and lose coherence.

## Action Steps

1. Pick one scene mood before style selection.
2. Check whether face contrast remains clear in that scene.
3. Reject options that force heavy compensation in hair/outfit.
