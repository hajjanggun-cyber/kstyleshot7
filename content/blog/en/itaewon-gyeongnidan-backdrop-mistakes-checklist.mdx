## Intro

Most backdrop errors happen before generation starts. A short pre-check prevents expensive retries.

## Why This Checklist Helps

Itaewon and Gyeongnidan-gil scenes have a **mixed global layers** signature. The checklist below keeps that mood while protecting face readability.

## 7 Mistakes to Avoid

1. Picking a backdrop with no target mood keyword
2. Using outfit contrast that fights the background density
3. Ignoring face light while chasing bright scenery
4. Over-cropping forehead or chin before export
5. Mixing inconsistent camera distances in one session
6. Leaving strong clutter behind hair edges
7. Finalizing without side-by-side crop comparison

## Fast Fix Sequence

- Set one mood keyword
- Validate hair readability
- Rebalance outfit contrast
- Fix framing and horizon
- Re-check with two crops

## Quick Checklist

- Mood keyword defined
- Hair and background separated
- Outfit contrast controlled
- Face light stable
- Final crop comparison done
