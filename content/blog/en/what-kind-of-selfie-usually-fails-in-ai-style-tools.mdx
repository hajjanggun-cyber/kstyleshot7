## Intro

Most failed results do not come from the style direction. They start with a weak source image.

## Why This Matters

If you know what usually fails, you can fix the input before spending time on the rest of the flow.

## 4 Risky Selfie Types

### 1. Dark indoor selfies

Low detail in the face makes the baseline harder to read.

### 2. Extreme close-up selfies

Too little distance creates distortion that carries into the result.

### 3. Heavy filtered selfies

Strong beauty filters remove useful detail and can confuse the natural structure of the face.

### 4. Busy background selfies

Visual clutter competes with the face and makes the input less clean.

## Common Mistakes

- Uploading the first available selfie
- Keeping strong filters on
- Ignoring lighting because the face looks “clear enough”

## Quick Checklist

- Face well lit
- Enough camera distance
- Minimal filters
- Clean background
