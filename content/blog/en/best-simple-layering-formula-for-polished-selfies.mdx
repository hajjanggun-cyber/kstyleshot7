## Intro

The Best Simple Layering Formula for Polished Selfies targets users searching cleaner outfit structure for selfie-based AI portrait styling.

## Quick Summary

- Prioritize silhouette before detail.
- Keep layering readable in front-camera framing.
- Match outfit density to background complexity.

## Why This Matters for Styling Conversion

Outfit clarity helps users choose faster and reduces second-guessing in later steps. Clean layering supports face-first composition and improves perceived quality.

## Action Steps

1. Choose one base layer and one supporting layer.
2. Check shoulder line and neckline against your crop.
3. Avoid adding detail if the background is already busy.
