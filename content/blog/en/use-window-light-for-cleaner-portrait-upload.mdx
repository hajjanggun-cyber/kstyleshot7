## Intro

Good lighting fixes more upload problems than most editing tricks.

## Why This Matters

Window light is soft, directional, and predictable. That makes it one of the easiest ways to improve a selfie before upload.

## 4 Practical Tips

### 1. Face the window, not the ceiling light

Front light from a window usually keeps skin tone and facial detail clearer than overhead bulbs.

### 2. Stand close enough to keep the light soft

If you stand too far away, the light gets weaker and the face can lose definition.

### 3. Avoid mixed color temperatures

Turn off strong yellow lamps if they fight the natural light and muddy the image.

### 4. Watch for hard shadows on one side

If one side of the face falls too dark, adjust your angle before taking the photo.

## Common Mistakes

- Using a bright window behind you
- Mixing daylight with warm indoor light
- Shooting when the room is still too dark

## Quick Checklist

- Window in front of you
- Face evenly lit
- No heavy yellow cast
- Eyes and jaw clearly visible
