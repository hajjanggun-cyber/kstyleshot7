## Intro

If you have never used a guided AI style flow before, the steps can feel more complicated than they really are.

## Why This Matters

Knowing the order of each step helps you prepare a better selfie, make faster choices, and avoid wasting time inside the tool.

## The Full Flow

### 1. Start with checkout

You begin by unlocking one session. The checkout step is separate so the styling flow can stay focused and short once you enter.

### 2. Upload one clean selfie

A centered, front-facing image gives the tool the best baseline. If you need a refresher, read [what makes a good selfie](/blog/en/what-makes-a-good-selfie-for-ai-style-generation).

### 3. Pick hair directions first

Hair usually changes the whole portrait mood fastest, so it is the first style decision in the flow.

### 4. Move into outfit and background

Once the face framing feels right, outfit and location choices are easier to judge because they build on the same mood.

### 5. Review and download

The last step is for checking the final look, downloading your result, and clearing your session if needed.

## Common Mistakes

- Uploading before checking lighting
- Picking random styles without a target mood
- Expecting every step to preserve the exact original face

## Quick Checklist

- Finish checkout first
- Prepare one clear selfie
- Choose one mood before selecting styles
- Review the final image before download
