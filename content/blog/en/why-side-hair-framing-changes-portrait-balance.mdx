## Intro

Why Side Hair Framing Changes the Whole Portrait Balance is about reducing hairstyle decision noise. Hair choices work best when you set mood, face framing, and camera context together.

## Why This Matters

Hair is the highest-impact style variable in this flow. If hair direction is unclear, outfit and backdrop choices become harder to evaluate.

## Practical Framework

1. Pick one hair mood first (clean, soft, or textured).
2. Match side framing to your camera angle.
3. Compare only two close variants before final selection.

## Common Mistakes

- Mixing opposite hair moods in one round
- Ignoring side-hair framing around cheeks and jawline
- Choosing based only on trend words instead of face balance
