## Intro

Hangang Park portraits look best when hairstyle shape matches the scene mood before outfit details are finalized.

## Why Hair Comes First Here

This backdrop has a **open river light and relaxed depth** tone. A hairstyle with **natural flow with low-maintenance volume** keeps the face readable while preserving that mood.

## Top 10 Hair Directions

1. Soft curtain bangs with light separation
2. Clean center part with controlled top volume
3. Side part with cheek-line framing
4. Mid-length layers with tidy ends
5. Airy fringe with natural forehead exposure
6. Low-frizz wave texture for movement
7. Lightweight hush-cut inspired silhouette
8. Blunt outline with softened inner layers
9. Ear-reveal style for sharper jawline reading
10. Balanced root lift for front-camera clarity

## Styling Notes for Hangang Park

- Keep volume near crown and side line, not random wide expansion.
- Avoid heavy fringe density that hides brow and eye balance.
- Test one cleaner and one softer variant before final choice.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing trend keywords without checking face-line readability
- Over-texturizing ends until jawline definition disappears
- Copying runway looks that are too heavy for selfie framing

## Quick Checklist

- Pick one hair mood keyword
- Keep face contour visible
- Control fringe density
- Compare two volume levels
- Finalize hair before outfit/background tweaks
