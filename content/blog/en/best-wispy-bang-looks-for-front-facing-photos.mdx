## Intro

Wispy bangs are one of the easiest ways to soften a front-facing portrait without making it feel heavy.

## Why This Matters

The fringe changes how the forehead, eyes, and cheek line are framed in the camera.

## 4 Practical Tips

### 1. Keep the fringe light

A wispy bang works because it leaves enough space for the face to breathe.

### 2. Do not block the eyes

The cleaner the eye area looks, the more balanced the selfie usually feels.

### 3. Let the ends blend softly

A softer transition into the side hair looks more natural in portraits.

### 4. Check it in front light

Heavy top lighting can make a light bang look denser than it is.

## Common Mistakes

- Cutting the fringe too thick
- Making the center too closed
- Leaving the texture too blunt

## Quick Checklist

- Light fringe density
- Eyes visible
- Soft side blend
- Front lighting
